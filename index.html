Performance Skills

I started to take piano lessons when i was 9 years old, and have been playing ever since. In my gradeschool years i picked up the trumpet as a part of band class, and play that to this day as well.
In highschool i eventually joined both marching band, and jazz band, in addition to our Orchestral band. Though I primarily stuck with trumpet, the skillset and love led to me to try multiple other brass instruments, such as trombone, and the french horn. I never quite fell in love with read instruments the same way as brass, however i did take the time to learn enough to get in trouble on the sax. The same is true of string instruments, of which i have dabbled in guitar, mandolin, and violin.
In addition to my love of music, I also have a passion for acting. I have been in multiple plays and musicals. The favorites of which were Sancho Panza in "Man of La Mancha", and Huck Finn in "The adventures of Tom Saywer". I also have a love of improv, and have been a part of multiple improv groups.
Though these are largely passion driven skills. The skills i have learned from them have been invaluable. I have learned how to work as a team, as well as a fleshed out skillset of social skills.
